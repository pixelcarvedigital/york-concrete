About York Concrete

Edmonton's Concrete
Specialists

Founded by Navtej Bajwa, York Concrete has grown into one of Edmonton's most trusted names in concrete construction. We combine craftsmanship, modern techniques, and local expertise to deliver results that stand the test of Alberta's demanding climate.

  • Comprehensive solutions for residential homeowners and commercial developers across Edmonton & area.

  • Climate-rated mixes — our concrete formulations are engineered specifically for Alberta freeze-thaw cycles.

  • Fully licensed & insured with transparent pricing and no hidden costs on every project.
